A fraction represents a part of a whole. When you break something into equal parts, each part is a fraction!

Think of a pizza cut into 8 equal slices. If you eat one slice, you've eaten 1/8 of the pizza.

  • The top number (1) is the Numerator: It tells us how many parts we have.
  • The bottom number (8) is the Denominator: It tells us how many equal parts the whole is divided into.
